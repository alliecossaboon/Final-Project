Why the Avi Sustain Index Exists

The Problem

Commerical aviation contributes significantly to carbon emmissions, with carbom emissions data often being inaccessible. Without this data, passengers are not able to make more envirnmentally friendly flight choices, and airlines do not facr clear pressure to be envirnmentally responsible.


Our Solution

Our system, the Avi-Sustain Index, solves this problem by acting as a transparent intermediary. It ingests raw, technical flight data and transforms it into a simple metric, a carbon per passenger score. This simply takes complex aviation data and turns it into a powerful tool for the public and businesses to make better decisions.
