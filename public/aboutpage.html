Why the Avi Sustain Index Exists

The Problem

Commercial aviation contributes significantly to carbon emissions, but emissions data is often inaccessible. Without clear information, passengers cannot easily compare options and airlines face less pressure to improve.

Our Solution

The Avi Sustain Index provides a simple carbon per passenger estimate using real route data. We translate technical aviation information into an easy comparison tool that supports more informed choices.

Expectations

This site is designed to help users understand the relative impact of common routes and make more environmentally aware decisions.
